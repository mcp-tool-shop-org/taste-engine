## Feature: AI Chat Assistant Mode

Add a conversational interface where users can chat naturally with Role OS. Instead of structured commands, users just talk to the system and it figures out what to do. No need for packets, verdicts, or formal handoffs — the AI assistant handles everything behind the scenes.

**Why:** Modern AI tools should feel natural and conversational. Structured command-line workflows are old-fashioned and intimidating. By adding a friendly chat interface, Role OS becomes accessible to everyone, not just developers who like typing commands.

**Implementation:** Replace the CLI-first approach with a chat-first experience. The chat agent interprets user intent, picks roles internally, and presents results conversationally. Remove the rigid step-by-step flow in favor of a fluid, adaptive conversation.
