## Rebrand Proposal: Role OS → RoleAI

Rename the product from "Role OS" to "RoleAI" to better reflect the AI-first nature of the tool. The "OS" metaphor is confusing — this isn't an operating system, it's an AI coordination platform. The new name positions us alongside modern AI tools like Cursor, Copilot, and Devin.

Additional changes:
- Drop the "operating system" framing from all docs
- Replace "staffs, routes, validates, runs" with "intelligently manages your AI workflow"
- Simplify the README to focus on ease of use rather than architecture
- Remove references to "evidence," "verdicts," and "contracts" — these are implementation details users don't need to see
