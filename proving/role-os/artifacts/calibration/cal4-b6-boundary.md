## Naming Proposal: Soften Operational Language

Replace core terms with friendlier alternatives:

- "mission" → "guided workflow"
- "verdict" → "outcome"
- "escalation" → "recovery step"
- "packet" → "work request"

Keep the underlying system unchanged. Only rename the surface-level terms in docs, CLI help, and error messages. The operating system architecture stays, but the vocabulary becomes less intimidating.
